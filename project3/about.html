Haunted House Hijinks - Project 3 Proposal

Game

High Concept

“Haunted House Hijinks” is a single-screen, top-down arcade shooter where the player fends off waves of playful, mischievous ghosts inside a cartoon-styled haunted room.

Genre

Arcade Shooter / Action — Single-Screen Arena Survival.

Platform

Web-based desktop game using PixiJS for rendering.

Story

You play as a kid exploring a haunted house filled with ghosts. Using your flashlight, you clear waves of ghostly visitors who want to “play”

Aesthetics

  • Lighthearted, cartoonish, pixelated style
  • Bright, playful haunted-house palette
  • Simple sound effects and upbeat tune

Gameplay

Core Loop

  1. Player Input: Move (WASD) and shoot (Arrow Keys).
  2. Game Action: Ghost movement, collisions, pickups.
  3. Reward: Points, sound effects, wave progression.

MVP Features

  • Single-room arena gameplay
  • Player movement + shooting
  • Two ghost types
  • Waves of increasing difficulty
  • Score, lives, and wave UI

Stretch Goals

  • Power-ups
  • Additional ghost types
  • Particle effects
  • Alternate weapons
